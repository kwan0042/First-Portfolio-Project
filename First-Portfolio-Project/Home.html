My Story

Hi, I am Daniel. I was born and raised in Hong Kong. I enjoy being a photographer, graphic designer, and traveler.

At University. I studied advertising and marketing management. I found design and filming. I like the design because everything that is around us has been designed and this has always fascinated me, I’ve wanted to be involved and a part of this.

Therefore, I taught myself design in my spare time. In my extracurricular time, I try to help other companies made promotional videos. When I can go from idea to shooting, from shooting to editing, and then from editing to production. I feel great satisfaction. Afterward, I self-study shooting, editing, and post-production.

Filming and photography had become my designated actions when traveling. Every time I look at the photos. The memory pushes me to embark on a trip again.
